July 2024 Meetings

Notice of Cancellation and Date Change of the Audit and Compliance and Health Equity and Diversity Committee Meetings & Notice of Meeting of the Board of Trustees

The MetroHealth System Board of Trustees is issuing this notice for a change in the Calendar of Scheduled meetings: to cancel the Audit & Compliance and Health Equity & Diversity Committee meetings scheduled for June 26, 2024, and to reschedule on a different date and a notice of a special meeting of the Board of Trustees. 

 

Board of Trustees

Wednesday, July 31, 2024
3:30 - 5:30 pm

Special Meeting - The purpose of this meeting is to approve the Internal Audit plan. It is anticipated that the majority of the meeting will take place in executive session.

Brooklyn Heights Campus, 5410 Lancaster Drive, Building B, Brooklyn Heights, OH 44131 - Directions

or Join via Zoom

 

 

Audit & Compliance Committee

Wednesday, July 31, 2024 (changed from June 26, 2024)
11:00 am – 1:00 pm

Brooklyn Heights Campus, 5410 Lancaster Drive, Building B, Brooklyn Heights, OH 44131 - Directions

or Join via Zoom

 

Executive Committee

Notice of a Special Meeting of the Board of Trustees Executive Committee

The MetroHealth System Board of Trustees is issuing this notice of a meeting of The Board of Trustees Executive Committee on July 1, 2024.

The purpose of the Executive Committee meeting is to discuss hospital trade secrets as defined by ORC 1333.61, to consider the appointment, employment, dismissal, discipline, promotion, demotion, or compensation of a public employee, and to conference with the public body’s attorney to discuss disputes involving the public body that are the subject of pending or imminent court action.

Please note that it is anticipated that the majority of the meeting will take place in executive session.

Monday, July 1, 2024
3:00 am – 5:00 pm

DESCRIPTION OF BOARD AND RESPONSIBILITIES:
The REDSS Governing Board is an entity of the Cuyahoga County Executive intended to provide guidance in the operation, maintenance, and enhancement to ​Cuyahoga County's Criminal Justice Regional Information System initiative.

QUALIFICATIONS:
Members serve at the virtue of their positions. Please see the enabling legislation for more information.

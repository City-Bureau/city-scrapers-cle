DESCRIPTION OF BOARD AND RESPONSIBILITIES:
The CCESAB was established by the government of County of Cuyahoga, to advise the County Executive and Cuyahoga County on Homeland Security and Public Safety matters in relation to enhancing prevention, protection, response, mitigation, and recovery capabilities, and to make this improved capacity available to municipal corporations and townships pursuant to Section 307.15 of the Ohio Revised Code.


QUALIFICATIONS:
The CCESAB is composed of nineteen (19) members representing various public safety and public health entities throughout the County.  Members of the CCESAB are assigned to the Board by their parent agency.

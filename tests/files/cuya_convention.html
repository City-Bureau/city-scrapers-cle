2019 Scheduled Board Meetings: January 25th, May 9th, July 26th and October 25th. All meetings begin at 8:00 am and are located in the GCHI Executive Board Room.

The Cuyahoga County Convention Facilities Development Corporation (CCCFDC) is a nonprofit formed by the Cuyahoga County Council for the purpose of promoting the revitalization of Greater Cleveland/ Cuyahoga County and enhancing the economic growth and employment opportunities in the area on behalf of the County.

The CCCFDC oversees the operation and management of the Huntington Convention Center of Cleveland and the Global Center for Health Innovation.

In addition to overseeing operation and management of County-owned assets, the CCCFDC and its board of directors approves the annual budget appropriation for each facility and works closely with the management team, SMG, hired by Cuyahoga County.

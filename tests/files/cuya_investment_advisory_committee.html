DESCRIPTION OF BOARD AND RESPONSIBILITIES:
The Cuyahoga County Investment Advisory Committee, as required by O.R.C Section 135.341, oversees the Cuyahoga County Government's investment management policies. The Committee ensures that county investment practices are comprehensive and inclusive and that all investment practices and instruments are clearly authorized under said stated policies.


QUALIFICATIONS:
Members serve at the virtue of their positions with Cuyahoga County. Please see the enabling legislation for more information.

MEETING SCHEDULE/AGENDAS:
The Cuyahoga County Investment Advisory Committee meets at least once every three months. Any member may call a meeting giving five days notice. Meetings are usually held on Thursday in January, April, July and October at 2079 E. 9th Street, Cleveland, Ohio 44115. Please contact the board for more information on the meeting schedule.

Board Meeting Materials Archive

Cuyahoga Arts & Culture is a public entity and therefore is subject to Ohio Sunshine Law. Once approved, minutes of the meetings of CAC's Board of Trustees, as well as other related materials, are posted here and available for download. All meetings of CAC's Board of Trustees are open to the public; see the list of upcoming Board meetings for more detail.

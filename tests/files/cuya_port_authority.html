Board of Directors Meetings

All meetings will begin at 8:30 a.m. and will be held at the Port of Cleveland Offices located at 1100 W. 9th Street - Suite 100, Cleveland, OH  44113 unless otherwise indicated.   Surface lot and metered parking available nearby.

Meetings are subject to cancellation and change.  For further information, please contact the Port at 216-241-8004.
